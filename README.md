House of Health is built using a combination of modern and reliable technologies. The core platform is developed using Java for the backend, providing a robust and scalable environment. The frontend uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a user-friendly interface. Data management and meal plan generation are supported by SQL databases, ensuring secure storage and retrieval of user information. This combination of technologies makes the platform efficient, interactive, and highly responsive to user needs.

House of Health is your ultimate nutrition and wellness companion designed to help people achieve a healthier lifestyle. Our platform focuses on personalized meal planning, weight management, and overall wellness guidance. Whether your goal is to lose weight, maintain a balanced diet, or gain energy, House of Health creates accurate meal plans tailored to your individual needs, helping you make healthier choices every day.
